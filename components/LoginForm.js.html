¶ Registration

The registration form has 4 fields

 
    case(REGISTER):
      loginForm = t.struct({
        username: t.String,
        email: t.String,
        password: t.String,
        passwordAgain: t.String
      });
      options.fields['username'] = username;
      options.fields['email'] = email;
      options.fields['password'] = password;
      options.fields['passwordAgain'] = passwordAgain;
      break;
